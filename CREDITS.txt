CREDITS

Pipe's Formable Puppets — Flag Asset Credits

The following assets were used in the creation of flags included in this mod,
covering German and Italian fascist puppet flags, formable nation flags, and
all other flag variants. Wikimedia assets are sourced from Wikimedia Commons
unless otherwise noted. Modifications were made to some assets to fit the flag
designs.
